FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
+

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for peak season, and 1–3 months for off-peak. Early booking ensures better rates and availability.

What's included in your tour packages?
+

Our all-inclusive packages cover flights, hotel accommodations, airport transfers, guided tours, most meals, and travel insurance. Specific inclusions vary by package.

Do you offer custom itineraries?
+

Absolutely! We specialize in tailor-made travel experiences. Share your vision and we'll craft a personalized itinerary that matches your interests and budget.

What is your cancellation policy?
+

We offer flexible cancellation up to 30 days before departure for a full refund. Cancellations within 30 days may incur fees. Travel insurance covers unexpected changes.
